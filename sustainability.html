Sustainability at ESS

We hold ourselves to the same standards we apply for clients. This page outlines our own ESG strategy, SDG alignment, and approach to responsible business in the markets we serve.

Mission

Implementable solutions that protect communities, ecosystems, and investment returns.

To deliver implementable environmental and social solutions that protect communities, ecosystems, and investment returns in the markets that need them most — with field teams who are accountable for outcomes, not just outputs.

Vision

A world where international standards are genuinely applied, not just reported.

Where emerging market projects create measurable positive impact for local communities — and where African sustainability professionals lead the work that shapes their own countries' development.

SDG Alignment

Contributing to the Goals that matter in our markets.

7 SDG

Affordable & Clean Energy

Supporting wind, solar, and renewable energy projects across Africa and Central Asia with field-level HSEC management.

8 SDG

Decent Work & Economic Growth

Over 60% of ESS staff are nationals of the countries we work in — professional careers in sustainability on their own terms.

13 SDG

Climate Action

Carbon credit and green finance services that channel climate investment into the projects and communities where it is needed most.

15 SDG

Life on Land

Biodiversity and ecosystem assessments that go beyond compliance — protecting the natural capital on which communities depend.
