Understanding HSEC, ESG & Sustainability

HSEC

Risk management and compliance with international standards, frameworks, and guidelines. HSEC issues are usually managed by an HSEC team at the project or company level via an HSEC Management System.

ESG

Performance monitoring of ESG issues across multiple investees or assets. ESG is a financier term, and is focused on risk management and compliance with key investor standards to protect against reputational risk and financial loss.

Sustainability

Aspirational internationally-agreed objectives embodied in the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Organisations need to determine how they align with and integrate these objectives into their business model and activities.
